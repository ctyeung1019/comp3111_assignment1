Handball & Basketball

I love sports involved a ball like table tennis, baseball, softball especially handball and basketball!

You don't know what is handball?Check out the video below! I'am inside the video!!

I have been a handball player for 10 years since secondary 1 and we keep going to form a team to play in Hong Kong Handball League. In fact, i am one of the member of HKUST Handball Team and representing HKUST to play in any kind of handball competitions. In latest summer, our team flighted to Taipei to participate International Handball Tournament that held by National Taiwan University! During the tournament, we play against different university teams from Japan, Singapore, TaiWan and Hong Kong. It maybe the once in a life time experience for a player like me that is not representing Hong Kong.

However, handball is not a common sport in Hong Kong so when i play with my friends, usually is basketball which equiped me some basketball technique. That's why i also joined CSE basketball team to play against other departments for Computer Science students!

My Footprint

During the holidays, i would like to travel with my buddies!

TaiWan(Tai Chung)

Korea(Seoul)

Traveling around the world can expand our sight and explore different cultures but the most important thing is that it can release our pressure! As a citizen living in a stressful city Hong Kong and studying in Hong Kong University of Stress and Tension, maintaining mental health is extremely important to us and traveling definitely can relax our mind! That's the reason why I love it! So far i have been to two countries TaiWan and Korea.

The food there is delicious! Especially the night market of TaiWan, we can enjoy cheap and tasty food and play game in booths which just like a little carniva! Korea is a shopping paradise for me because all the clothes and shoes are much cheaper than Hong Kong even some famous brand! Besides, one thing i found in common in two countries are surprised me which is the closing time of shops is very early!!! After 10pm,only a liitle or none of shops and people are opening or on the streets! This is totally different to Hong Kong and we can only know this by traveling there in personal.That's the significance of traveling!
